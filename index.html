NEED FINDING AND PAPER PROTOTYPING

Published by: Hazzel Belanio

2nd week of USERDES class was fun. Ms. Jen gave us some suggestions on how we can improve and enhance the design we’ve created. We’ve also tackled about need finding. Need finding is the art of talking to people and discovering their needs both those they might explicitly state, and those hidden beneath the surface. It is only in truly understanding people that we can gain meaningful insights to inspire and inform a final, impactful design. It will help us with thinking of an application that will address issues and problems in this world.

Favorite Application

Published by: Hazzel Belanio

Flo is a menstruation, menopause tracking, ovulation calendar, and pregnancy monitoring mobile application. The application is available in IOS and Android. I like it because it tracks your period and ovulation. You’ll log symptoms and activities and the application will get the most accurate AI-based period and ovulation predictions. Flo application also share daily health insights like articles, quizzes and some surveys in your personal feed. If you’re pregnant or in post-pregnant modes, the application can track your baby’s development and you can learn the essentials of being a parent with special visuals and articles developed by medical experts.
